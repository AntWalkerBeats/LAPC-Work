Ant Walker

Hip Hop. Honest & Raw

Ant Walker is an artist hailing from the Washington, DC area who now resides in Greater Los Angeles. Ant Walker began his hip hop journey as a young rapper in the DMV's underground scene. During this time, he independently released the solo album War Journal along with several appearances alongside the collectives Poetic Justice League and Street Soliders.

Years after putting down the microphone, he began making beats as a hobby. What began as a constructive way to kill time reignited his love of the art. With production reminiscent of the boom bap sound that he grew up with, Ant Walker has contributed to releases from Ricky Ruckus, Juan Giovanni, and Hollywood NOP. He is also slated to appear on an upcoming project from DJ Battlecat.
